Safe & Loving

At Living In Victory Sober Living Missouri, Illinois we aim to provide a safe and loving place for residents to continue their journey in sobriety. We will guide our residents into becoming independent and productive members of the community and teach them how to live happier sober lifestyles.

Reconnect

We will help facilitate resources that gives them educational, charitable and social welfare activities. Our goal is to reconnect the men and women suffering from chemical dependency and other related disorders, with society and offer them a chance at complete recovery.

Staff

LIV Recovery Sober Living is staffed 24 hour with a Live In house manager. We foster the 12 step based, residential recovery program and provide in house recovery activities such as house meetings and recovery speakers. Our staff cares about you and your recovery!
